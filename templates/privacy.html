Slayhack

Privacy Policy

Slayhack uses connected platform access only to manage owned social media publishing workflows.

Information We Use

Slayhack may use account, Page, Instagram business account, publishing, and engagement information made available through Meta APIs after an authorized administrator grants access.

How We Use Information

We use this information to schedule or publish approved posts, confirm publishing status, and review basic performance signals for owned business accounts.

Storage And Access

Access tokens and operational records are stored in restricted production systems. Secrets are not committed to the source repository and are only used by the publishing services.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only with service providers needed to operate the publishing workflow or when required by law.

Deletion

You can request deletion of Slayhack app data by following the instructions on the data deletion page.

Contact

For privacy or data requests, contact the Slayhack administrator at the contact email configured in the Meta app dashboard.
