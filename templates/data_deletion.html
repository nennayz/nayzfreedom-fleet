Slayhack

Data Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of data connected to the Slayhack Meta app.

How To Request Deletion

Send a deletion request to the Slayhack administrator using the contact email configured in the Meta app dashboard. Include the Facebook Page name, Instagram business account username, and the email address used to grant app access.

What We Delete

After validating the request, we remove stored app access tokens, cached publishing metadata, and operational records associated with the requested account where retention is not legally required.

Timing

Deletion requests are reviewed and completed as soon as practical. If more information is needed to confirm ownership, the administrator will contact the requester.

Disconnecting Access

You can also remove Slayhack access directly from Meta account settings. Revoking access prevents future API access from the app.
