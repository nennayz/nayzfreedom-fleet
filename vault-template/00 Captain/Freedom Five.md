# Freedom Five

The Freedom Five are the core values of Captain Nayz:

1. **Work** — freedom in meaningful work and creation
2. **Wealth** — financial independence and wise stewardship
3. **Love** — relationships, belonging, and emotional richness
4. **Health** — physical and mental vitality
5. **Soul** — meaning, music, spirituality, and inner truth

## Reflection prompts

- Which freedom needs the most attention this week?
- Which freedom is strongest right now?
- What tradeoff is the Captain currently making?
