# 2026-05-15 — Ship Names and Freedom Five

**Status:** Accepted

## Context

Captain Nayz wanted ship names that felt classic and personally meaningful. The naming needed to support long-term identity, not just functional labels.

Captain Nayz also identified the core value system as the **Freedom Five**:

- Work
- Wealth
- Love
- Health
- Soul

## Decision

Use these ship names:

| Ship | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `The Aurora` | work, creation, brands, and moving toward beautiful horizons |
| `The Freedom` | personal life guided by the Freedom Five |
| `The Lyra` | music, stars, songs, and soul expression |

Use **The Horizon Atlas** as the travel/expedition program under `The Freedom` for now.

## Consequences

- The naming is values-based rather than purely functional.
- The Freedom becomes the personal anchor ship.
- The Lyra gives music its own identity instead of treating it as only a hobby.
- Travel remains a major program but not a separate ship yet.

## Follow-ups

- Revisit whether The Horizon Atlas becomes its own ship if travel becomes a major public creative/business line.
